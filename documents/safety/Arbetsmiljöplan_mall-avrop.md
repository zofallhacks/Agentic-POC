# Arbetsmiljöplan (Summary)

## About
This document is a template for an **Arbetsmiljöplan (Work Environment Plan)** used in construction projects. It is created by the client (Bas-P) and later taken over and completed by the contractor (Bas-U).

## Purpose
- Identify risks in construction projects
- Define preventive measures
- Ensure safe coordination during execution

## Key Sections

### 1. Project Information
- Description of work
- Scope and location

### 2. Work Environment Organization
Includes:
- Client (Byggherre)
- Bas-P (planning coordinator)
- Bas-U (execution coordinator)
- Contractors
- Other responsible roles (e.g., fire safety, safety reps)

### 3. Coordination During Construction
- Regular safety inspections (skyddsronder)
- Coordination with ongoing operations
- Reporting of incidents, risks, and accidents
- Communication with facility management

### 4. Risks and Mitigation
The plan lists high-risk construction activities (based on AFS 2023:3), including:

#### Examples of Risks
1. Falls from height
2. Ground collapse / excavation risks
3. Handling heavy building elements
4. Exposure to hazardous substances (e.g., asbestos, PCB, quartz dust)
5. Demolition risks
6. Work near high-voltage lines
7. Traffic-related risks
8. Explosives and blasting
9. Underground work (tunnels, wells)
10. Drowning risks
11. Diving work
12. Work under pressure (caisson)
13. Radiation exposure
14. Work in active environments (shared sites)

#### General Mitigation Principles
- Prefer collective protection (rails, scaffolding)
- Avoid lone work in high-risk situations
- Ensure proper training and certification
- Conduct risk assessments before work
- Use protective equipment when needed
- Maintain clear communication and coordination

### 5. Continuous Updates
- The plan must be updated continuously as project planning evolves

---

## Notes
- The template includes placeholder text to be removed/updated
- Contractors must adapt the risks and measures to the actual project
- The plan must be accessible to all workers
